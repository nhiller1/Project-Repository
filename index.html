NB Aero

NB Aero's mission is to serve as a liaison between Admill Spolka z.o.o and its current and potential U.S. customers. This website showcases the capabilities and facilities of Admill Spolka z.o.o. and what sets us apart from other aerospace manufacturing companies. To learn more about the specific machines and capabilities of Admill z.o.o. click here.

Admill z.o.o. uses cutting edge technology to manufacture parts for commercial jet airplane engines, such as the Pratt & Whitney Airbus a320 neo engine shown in the background here. Admill z.o.o. produces a wide range of over 900 products, including parts made from stainless steel, nickel, titanium and aluminum alloys and is a supplier for Pratt & Whitney USA, Pratt & Whitney Canada, JPB Systeme, ITP Spain, and others. At NB Aero we pride ourselves on coordinating with Admill z.o.o. to ensure quality products and on time delivery to our customers. Our role is to facilitate the customer relationship and ensure Admill Spolka z.o.o. exceeds customer expectations for quality, delivery and design coordination.
