More About the U.S. Office

Our team in the U.S. office is your liason to the manfuacturing team in Poland. We work with the customer to define requirements, quote price and lead-time and define appropriate manufacturing processes to ensure a quality product that is on time and cost effective. Our quoting department is knowledgeable about raw material and special processes and will use this experience to ensure a thorough and accurate quote.

A successful quote submission results in a customer order. From there we will ensure thorough and timely communication with the customer regarding order processing, manufacturing status, and delivery status. We will also assit with the logistics of shipping and coordinate outside processes that are not available overseas, but can be done here in the U.S. We have a state-of-the-art order tracking system that ensures that an accurate delivery forecast is available at all times.
