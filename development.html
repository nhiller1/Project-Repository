New Product Development

Another area in which NB Aero stands apart from its peers is that we will assist in new product development. We will begin by developing and in-depth understanding of the design intent and the design constraints of the form, fit and function of the part. We will then review the design of the part and develop cost savings ideas from the manufacturing engineering perspective. This is service not offered by our peers.

We have equipment in the U.S. that is a duplicate of the equipment we have in the Admill z.o.o facility. The U.S. equipment will be utilized for developing new customer products and cost reduction initiatives. This gives us the ability to manufacture small lots (short run) of development parts for quick turn-around to the customer. This flexibility ensures that the project keeps moving from the onset. Once the development parts are approved, we can transition the work overseas (Admill z.o.o) for the high volume production process.

The ability to have a short turn around time using U.S. machinery for the first part approval while using Admill z.o.o for the high-volume will enable a more efficient and cost-effective way of the end-to-end process for our customer.
