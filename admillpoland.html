About Admill Poland

Company Capabilities:

Admill Spolka z.o.o is a world class supplier in the aerospace, space, and defense industries. They are a private company with Polish-American company. Since their start 12 years ago, they have grown to be a company of over 100 employees with over 900 different parts produced. They aspire to be the number one company in quality, delivery and value.

Facilities:

This is an image of the three different buildings that Admill z.o.o. has. Building A is 8,000 square feet and building B is 7,000 square feet. A recent addition to this company's resources is the 8,600 square foot building C. This is a brand new building with air-conditioned quality inspection. Because of how new it is, it can be adapted to the customer's particular requirements and specifications, regarding people, machines and products. For instance, this building can be utilized for total isolated military production. Overall, the addition of this building further expands the capabilities of Admill z.o.o.
